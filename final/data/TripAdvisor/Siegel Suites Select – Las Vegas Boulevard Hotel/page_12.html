A very budget hotel with no frills

This hotel caters to extended stay crowd that are on a very tight budget. Some guests have rented rooms for months at a time and call it home. The hotel is a good 2 miles south of Mandalay Bay and if you have a car it's not too bad to get around. If you are planning on staying here please note that the hotel only provides minimal room supplies. The kitchenette has a coffee maker, 1 pan and a place setting for 2- nothing else, not even soap to do dishes. Bathrooms have only a bar of soap and 2 towels. They do not provide any extra towels or amenities. My room was basic but bedding had cigerate burns in it. I heard children running in the halls quite often as well as rather load nieghbors. All in all it was an ok stay but would rather spend a few dollars more for something a bit nicer.
