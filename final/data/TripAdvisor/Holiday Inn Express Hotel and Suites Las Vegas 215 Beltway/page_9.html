Good option

I choose this hotel because the week i planned my vacations, there were some sort of convention and all the rooms in the strip were above $ 400.00 per room.The rooms at the HI express southbelt are clean and spaciosus. there are a shuttle betwen hotel and Mandalay Bay at 10.00 am, without return, so it`s necesary to rent a car. The duration of the trip betwen the hotel a to the bellagio took me 15 to 20 mins. in my rented car. There is a complementary breakfast with hardboiled eggs , yogourts, cinnamon rolls, etc. Just outside there is a good restaurant Black Jack, that opens 24 hrs. and serves breakfast, lunch and dinner (Good Option). Overall it was a good option due to the rates on the strip.
