Happy with the Hampton Inn

I recently spent four nights here, staying in two different rooms over the course of the same week. The first night was in a room with an exceptionally comfortable king sized bed with fluffy duvet and plenty of pillows to choose from. The room had a big screen TV, writing desk and great reading lamps, which I much appreciated. The bathroom was spacious and well appointed. It had a shower stall with sliding doors (no tub, which was fine with me), free standing vanity, plenty of towels, several towel/clothing hooks, nice amenities and a night light, which I always find helpful when staying in an unfamiliar place. This room was spotlessly clean, quiet , comfortable and everything I needed to recover from a series of international flights from SE Asia. The second stay was for three nights in a double room, which had two queen sized beds, a bathroom with shower and tub. The beds, although smaller, had the same comfortable mattresses, spotless bedding and several pillows. This room wasn't as thoroughly clean as the first (different housekeeper perhaps?) but was very comfortable just the same. The hotel is close to some shops, yet in a quiet area away from the chaos of Las Vegas. During the second stay, the hotel was experiencing some sewage back-up issues. The lobby, business center and most of the first floor smelled terrible for two days, but the hotel staff was aware of the problem and was trying to correct it. The breakfast was good and included in the rate (yogurt, bagels, toast, waffles, fruit, etc). The hotel offers tea and coffee throughout the day, which I thought was a nice touch.


Great place away from the Strip!

My husband and I stayed at this brand new Hampton for a quick trip to visit our son in Summerlin. We upgraded to a studio suite for an additional $20.00. The front desk staff was very friendly and checkin very quick. The room was quite spacious with a view of the Strip at night. The bed was wonderful and the room itself was spotlessly clean. Contemporary decorating, Hampton breakfast was included and is only about 10 - 15 minutes from the Strip on surface streets. It's also very close to many good restaurants. It has a little "convenience shop" in the lobby to get sodas, popcorn, ice cream or toiletries although those items are quite a bit more pricey than in a store. I would recommend this hotel to anybody that doesn't want to deal with the traffic and congestion of the Strip but wants a very convenient location in Vegas.

Hotel très correct dans quartier paisible

J'avais effectué un "request" préalable pour obtenir un lit simple supplémentaire qui n'a pas été disponible le premier soir mais à partir du deuxième jour et gratuitement chaque nuit suivante (une première !). Fin avril 2013 la piscine est propre mais l'eau à 18° environ, au soleil le matin, à l'ombre l'après midi. Situé à une dizaine de kilomètre du strip par autoroute ou Flamingo road.
